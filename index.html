Pep Pets

The Team

My name is Kathy, and this Pep Pets.

A Pep Pet is a handheld toy pet that you can take care of by becoming more active and attentive. For children, it's a fun new game that they can interact with their friends through. For parents, it's a way to teach their children responsibility without all the worry of a real pet or phone.
